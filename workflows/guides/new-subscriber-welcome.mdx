---
title: New subscriber welcome
description: Reduce early churn by showing value-first content and offering a choice of incentives.
---

New subscribers are in the **highest churn risk** window. A strong onboarding workflow helps them understand the value of your product and builds the habit that drives long-term retention.

## Goal

Decrease churn for fresh subscribers.

## Recommended setup

- **Trigger**: Customer Portal Entered

### Key actions

- **Show Content**: explain value with images/video (what to expect, how to use, results timeline)
- **Offer Selection** *(optional)*: let customers choose between a discount or a free product for the next order

### Conditions to consider

Use lifecycle conditions to target the right cohort:

- `subscription.cycle_count`
- `subscription.age.days`

Examples:

- Show onboarding content only if `cycle_count = 1` or `age.days < 7`
- Offer incentives only after the customer has seen value content

## Best practices

- **Lead with value**: incentives work best after the customer understands the benefit.
- **Keep it short**: one strong piece of content beats a long sequence.
- **Record outcomes**: tag customers who accepted an offer for later analysis.
