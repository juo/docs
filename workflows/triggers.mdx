Triggers are events that **start a workflow**.

This page lists **only triggers that are launched**.

## Customer portal triggers

- **Customer portal entered**: customer logs into the subscription portal
- **Skip clicked**: customer chooses to skip an upcoming order
- **Pause clicked**: customer pauses their subscription
- **Cancellation clicked**: customer starts the cancellation process
- **Renew now clicked**: customer initiates a “renew now” action
- **Product subscription cancellation**: customer initiates cancellation for a specific subscription product

## System triggers

- **Schedule order**: system schedules an order
- **Payment failed**: a billing attempt fails
