This guide is **coming soon**.

In the meantime, here’s the intended shape of a winback workflow:

- **Trigger**: Subscription cancelled / churned *(system event)*
- **Delay**: wait \(X\) days
- **Show Content**: remind the customer of value + what’s new
- **Offer Selection**: choose a winback incentive (discount vs free product)
- **Confirmation**: success vs no action
