---
title: Actions
description: Steps that a workflow performs (interactions and executions).
---

Actions define **what the workflow does once it starts**.

After a trigger fires and conditions are evaluated, actions determine what happens next. A workflow can contain multiple actions and can branch based on customer responses.

## Action categories

- **Interactions**: show something to the customer and **wait for a response**
- **Executions**: run automatically without requiring customer input

This page lists **only actions that are launched**.

## Interactions

Interactions are commonly used in the **Customer Portal** and can branch based on what the customer selects.

- **Custom offer** 
- **Offer skip order**
- **Offer free product**
- **Offer discount**
- **Offer selection**
- **Offer swap product**
- **Poll**
- **Offer renew**
- **Offer change frequency**
- **Show content**
- **Show confirmation**

## Executions

Execution actions run automatically, typically to update the subscription or record state.

- **Skip order**
- **Apply discount**
- **Change frequency**
- **Remove all discounts**
- **Cancel subscription**
- **Add product**
- **Swap product**
