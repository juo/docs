## Management options

In this section, you can decide which options are available to your subscribers in the Customer Portal. If certain options are unchecked, subscribers will need to contact support for changes.

![Global settings view - Management options](https://cdn.juo.io/content-uploads/settings_management_options_671659741b.png)

## Products editing

This section lets you specify what products editing options will be available to your subscribers.

## Excluded products

By default, your customers can add all your active products to their subscriptions. This section allows you to exclude specific products from being available in the Customer Portal.
